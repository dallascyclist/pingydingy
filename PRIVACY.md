# Privacy Policy

**PingyDingy — Network Monitor**
**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

## Summary

PingyDingy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** Specifically:

- No personal information
- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No usage tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies
- No device fingerprinting

## Data Storage

All data created by PingyDingy — host configurations, ping results, and logs — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's export feature.

## Network Activity

PingyDingy sends ICMP echo requests (pings) and TCP connection requests to hosts that **you** configure. These are standard network diagnostic packets directed only at addresses you specify. No data is sent to us or any third party.

## Config Sharing

The config sharing feature (`.pingydingy` files via AirDrop) transfers host configuration data directly between devices using Apple's peer-to-peer protocols. No data passes through our servers — we don't have servers.

## Third-Party Services

PingyDingy uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, ad networks, or cloud services. The app is built entirely with Apple's first-party frameworks.

## Open Source

PingyDingy is open source. You can inspect exactly what the app does:
https://github.com/dallascyclist/pingydingy

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
